Excerpts on Mr Peter Gregory Obi

  • July 1961: born in Onitsha, a city in Anambra state
  • In 1984: graduated from the department of Philosophy from University of Nigeria Nsukka
  • In 2006: became the governor of Anambra state, and he governed till 2014
  • In 2014: He was honoured with a papal knight of St. Sylvester by Pope Francis
  • 2019: He was the running mate of Atiku Abubakar prior to the 2019 presidential elections
  • 2022: Became the candidate of Labour Party for the presidential elections come 2023.
